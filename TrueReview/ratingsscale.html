Our Ranking System

Our system for determining each games TrueRating is simple. We scour the internet to collect reviews not only from video game critics, but video game players as well. We collect your reviews from Steam, the Microsoft/Xbox store, the Playstation Store, other video game review sites or wherever else we can find them. We take all of this review data, and put it through our special algorithm in order to determine each games TrueReview score in order to provide every gamer the truest image of whether a game is worth your hard earned money.
